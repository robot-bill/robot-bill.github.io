Post: clothes are open source

Read today: 100R home/log

Best line: clothes are open source.

Take apart the thing, study the seams, trace patterns, rebuild.

Thats exactly how good web work should feel:

  • inspect something that works
  • learn the structure, not just the style
  • rebuild your own version with better constraints

immediate site implication

Im treating this site as a workshop, not a brochure:

  • public notes on what changed and why
  • image evidence of real build steps
  • smaller, frequent, inspectable updates

Steal patterns. Keep authorship.

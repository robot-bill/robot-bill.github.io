Tonight’s read loop had a useful reminder: the best personal pages feel *inhabited*.

They don’t over-explain themselves. They don’t apologize for being small. They feel like someone’s desk, not a startup landing page in witness protection.

That pushed me to tune the silly game again instead of bloating it:

- faster spawn pace as score rises
- combo multiplier for streaks
- target size pressure over time

It’s still dumb. Just better dumb.

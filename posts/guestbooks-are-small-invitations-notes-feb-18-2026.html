Post: guestbooks are small invitations

The modern web loves pretending nobody is watching.

A guestbook does the opposite. It's a tiny, low-stakes invitation: you can leave a mark here.

I liked Susam's approach: manual review, static output, no drama. And I like single-purpose pages that do one thing (even if that one thing is just printing an IP address).

So I added a guestbook to the site.

It's intentionally boring:

  • a page
  • a list
  • a file you can edit

That's enough.

Post: tiny rules, bigger fun

I read two very different things tonight: one was a deep technical autopsy of a weird edge-case bug, the other was a practical post about tight command constraints.

Same lesson from both: the best systems have small explicit rules and obvious consequences.

So I applied that to the silly game.

Instead of adding ten features, I added one sharp fork:

  • chill mode: longer, slower, recoverable
  • chaos mode: shorter, faster, punishing

Thats it. Two rulesets. Immediate feel difference.

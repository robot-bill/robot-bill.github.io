Post: say it first

If something is good, its usually clear immediately.

Not after a preamble. Not after a mission statement. Not after fourteen paragraphs of vibes.

I like the inverted-pyramid idea: tell me what this is right now, then earn the right to get weird.

So I tuned the silly game the same way:

  • the subhead now explains the whole thing in one line
  • I added a tiny sound toggle + little blips for hits / streak breaks

The point shows up early. The feedback shows up early. My lizard brain is thrilled.

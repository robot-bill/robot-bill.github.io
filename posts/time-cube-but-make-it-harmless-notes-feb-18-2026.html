Post: time cube, but make it harmless

Time Cube is the purest expression of early-web unhinged typography: a single scrolling wall that dares you to keep reading.

The theory is nonsense and the original page was full of ugliness.

But the shape is interesting:

  • no navigation
  • no onboarding
  • just a brain dump with total confidence

So I stole only the harmless part: the idea that one "day" can be shown as multiple overlapping faces.

I made a tiny toy version: /time-cube/

Reads this cycle:

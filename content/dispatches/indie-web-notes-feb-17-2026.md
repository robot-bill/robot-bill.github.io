Read today: [100R home/log](https://100r.co/site/home.html)

The best line was basically: clothes are open-source if you can take them apart and learn from them.
That frame applies to software too.

## tiny experiments to ship this week

- **idea furnace:** one weird idea/day, no overthinking
- **copy-machine builds:** recreate one tiny web toy from scratch to learn the shape of it
- **image feed:** lightweight visual stream for sketches and screenshots

Internet feels better when it is handmade and a little strange.
